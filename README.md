# Cmaa Snacks — Brand Hub

Brand identity + website exploration for **Cmaa Snacks** — homemade Chakli & Chivda, made by a real mother. *"Cmaa" ≈ "Seema" + "Maa".*

## What's here
- **`/vote/`** — the **Team Voting Board**: vote 👍/👎 and add feedback on names, logos, taglines, colours, flavours, packaging and more. Shows who voted on what.
- **`/studio/logos.html`** — Logo Studio: 9 refined logo concepts (3 families) with colour / reversed / favicon variants.
- **`/moodboard/`** — the original brand & design mood board (palettes, type, 6 art directions).
- **`/assets/`** — real product imagery (credited, Creative Commons — see `assets/images/ATTRIBUTIONS.md`) and logo SVGs.
- **`/research/`** — FMCG/web design, brand strategy, product, tech and art-direction research.

All concepts are early drafts on a blank slate — nothing is final.
