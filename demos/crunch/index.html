Meet Seema 👋

People kept asking for "one more box." So I made one more. Then a thousand more. I'm Seema — a mum from Pune who never trusted a packet of snacks in her life. Every Diwali my kitchen turned into a chakli factory of one, and my kids' friends would loiter near the door waiting for the first hot batch.

That's the whole secret. I press every chakli by hand, fry small so nothing sits, and use the ghee I'd feed my own family. "Cmaa" is just how my little one said my name — it's got Maa right in it. That's the promise in every tin: snacks made the way a mother would make them. Crunchy, honest, and a little bit addictive.

— Seema 🧡
