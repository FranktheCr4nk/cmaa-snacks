HOW THE PRESS WORKS

चार चित्रों में पूरी कहानी
FIG. 1

Soak & grind. Seven grains bathe overnight; at dawn they are ground into bhajani, the house flour.

FIG. 2

Knead. The dough is worked until it confesses softness. No shortcuts, no maida, no mercy.

FIG. 3

Press. The brass press turns its screw and the dough surrenders in perfect spirals.

FIG. 4

Fry. Groundnut oil, morning shift only. Golden means done; Maa’s ear knows the sound.

Public Notice

Standing orders of the proprietor · In force since the first batch

Clause 1 —

No batch leaves this kitchen before Maa has eaten from it. Quality control is a spoon.

Clause 2 —

Afternoon oil is tired oil. Morning batches only. The press keeps bakery hours.

Clause 3 —

If a chakli does not snap in one clean break, the whole tray is demoted to family snacks. The family does not complain.

Clause 4 —

Besan is weighed. Love is not. Both are added generously, in that order.

GRIEVANCE WINDOW

लाइन में लगिए, जवाब पक्का मिलेगा
WINDOW №1Why is there no maida in anything?+

Because Maa said so, and the press does not argue with the proprietor. Everything is built on bhajani — a seven-grain flour ground in-house. It tastes better, sits lighter, and wins every argument by default.

WINDOW №2How long does a tin stay fresh?+

Three weeks in an airtight tin, away from sunlight and siblings. In field tests conducted by our readers, no tin has survived past day four.

WINDOW №3Where do you deliver, and what does it cost?+

Pune in 1–2 days, the rest of India in 3–5. Dispatch is ₹49 flat, and free on orders above ₹499 — the press believes in round numbers and full tins.

WINDOW №4Can I book the Faral Box for Diwali gifting?+

Yes — and please do it early. Festive tins are pressed strictly in order of booking, and the list closes on 25 October. Corporate orders welcome; the press enjoys impressing your boss.

WINDOW №5Is it really homemade, or "homemade"?+

One kitchen, one brass press, one Maa. Small batches mean we sometimes sell out by noon — inconvenient for you, but the queue is the proof.
