Meet Seema

The hands behind every spiral.

Cmaa began at a kitchen counter in Pune, where Seema — a mother of two — pressed chakli the way her own Aai taught her: slowly, by hand, one perfect spiral at a time. When her children moved abroad, she'd post them tins of faral so a bite of Diwali always reached them on time.

Friends asked for theirs. Then friends of friends. Today Cmaa is still that same kitchen, scaled with love and never machines — fresh ghee, real spices, no shortcuts. "Cmaa" carries her name, and the Maa inside it. Every box leaves with a blessing.

— SeemaFounder · Head Cook · Maa
