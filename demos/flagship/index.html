The Morning Batchपहली घाणी

01
04:30 — soak & grind

Soak & grind

Rice, chana, urad — soaked overnight, ground while the street is still dark. Fresh bhajani smells like rain landing on a hot stone.

02
05:40 — press

Press the spirals

One steady wrist-turn per chakli, straight into the kadhai. Machines press circles; hands press spirals with a beginning and an end.

03
06:15 — fry

Ghee-fry & listen

Into ghee that is hot but never angry. The oil ripples, the steam climbs, and Seema pulls each spiral at the first deep gold — onto old newspaper, the way it has always been done.

04
07:00 — pack

Packed while it crackles

Weighed twice, tasted once, sealed before the oil cools. The batch number is inked by hand — if it’s SB-11, Seema pressed it herself.
