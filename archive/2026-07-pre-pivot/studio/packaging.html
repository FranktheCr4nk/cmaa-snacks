02 — Label Flats · V1 archive

Two directions, side by side

The same information architecture — brand band, bilingual product name, die-cut window, spice meter, veg mark, batch stamp, FSSAI & barcode — composed in two very different voices. Click any flat to open the SVG.

Direction A — Warm Editorial

CREAM · GOLD · FRAUNCES

Cream paper, high-contrast serif and gold hairlines. Flavour is coded through the band colour and headline while every brand element stays fixed across the range — so the shelf reads as one warm, premium family. The arched window nods to old Pune storefront doorways.

Direction B — Kraft Press

KRAFT · INK · LETTERPRESS

One colour of ink on unbleached kraft, letterpress rules, mono details and a hand-stamped batch block. Flavour appears only as a single spot-colour band and a small diamond chip — honest, utilitarian, straight from the stamp room. The circular window feels like a tin viewing port.
