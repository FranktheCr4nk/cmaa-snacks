आजीचे नियम House Rules

Aaji doesn't own a measuring cup.

  • नियम १The dough tells you, not the clock.

    Bhajani rests until it stops sticking to the back of her hand. Some days twenty minutes. Some days she goes and waters the tulshi first.

  • नियम २Listen for the second hiss.

    The first hiss means the ghee is ready. The second — softer — means the chakli is. There is no thermometer in this kitchen and there never will be.

  • नियम ३The dabba never travels half-full.

    If a batch comes up short, she fries another. A half-full tiffin, she says, is a broken promise with a lid on it.

"चव घाईत येत नाही." — "Flavour doesn't come in a hurry." Seema aaji, stirring, not looking up.
