The Spread

फराळ थाळी

Six things, one kadhai, fried in aaji’s order.

In aaji’s building in Kothrud, Diwali faral never stayed home. The thali went up to the Joshis on the second floor. The steel dabba went across to Deshpande kaku. A parcel went to Sadashiv Peth by the 4 o’clock bus.

And the dabba always came back empty. That is the entire quality report.

She fries in the order her mother fried: chakli first — the ghee must be new — chivda last, when the kadhai is seasoned and knows what it’s doing. Nothing waits overnight. Nothing is weighed twice.
